PA Skills Portable

LLM-powered PDF and bank-statement processing skills, packaged portably.

Launching

Run PASkillsPortable.exe. The app starts a local web UI and, unless disabled, opens it in your browser at http://127.0.0.1:<port>. The chosen port is written to ui_port.json next to the executable.

What it does

PA Skills converts bank statements (ICICI, HDFC, HSBC, Bank of Baroda and more) and tax documents into clean, canonical CSVs, and helps map them into GnuCash. Everything runs locally on your machine.

Language models (optional)

Deterministic skills (statement parsing, canonical conversion) need no LLM. Skills that use a model read their endpoint from config.yaml (default: a local Ollama server). No data leaves your machine unless you configure a remote endpoint yourself.

Data & settings

Your settings and outputs live under the app's Data folder, so the app stays self-contained and portable across machines.
