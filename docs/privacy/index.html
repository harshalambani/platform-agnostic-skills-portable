Privacy Policy

PA Skills Portable — a local, portable desktop application

Last updated: 10 July 2026

Short version: The App runs entirely on your own computer. The developer operates no server, no account system, and no analytics. The App does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal information. The only time your data leaves your machine is if you choose to connect the App to an external AI (LLM) endpoint — and even then it goes directly from your computer to that provider, never to the developer.

1. Who this policy is from

PA Skills Portable (“the App”) is an independent, non-commercial project distributed as source and as a portable build on GitHub. It is not operated as a hosted service. There is no company, no login, and no user database behind it.

2. The App runs locally

The App is a self-contained portable package. It requires no installation, no administrator rights, and makes no changes to the Windows registry. When you launch it, it starts a local web interface that is reachable only from your own computer (http://127.0.0.1). Your files are read, processed, and written back on your machine, to folders you choose.

3. Information the App does not collect

  • The App has no telemetry, analytics, tracking, or crash reporting.
  • The App does not create accounts and does not ask for names, addresses, passwords, or payment information.
  • The developer receives no copy of your documents, your extracted data, your settings, or any usage information. There is no server to receive it.

4. Data you provide, and where it stays

  • Documents and files you select (for example bank statements, PDFs, spreadsheets, emails) are processed locally and remain on your computer.
  • Settings — including any AI endpoint URL, model name, and API key you enter — are stored only in a local configuration file on your machine (Data/settings/config.yaml) so the App can reconnect next time. They are not transmitted to the developer. This file is stored in plain text on your own device; keep your machine secured accordingly.
  • PDF passwords you type to open protected files are used in-memory for that operation and are not saved.

5. Optional AI (LLM) processing — the one time data leaves your machine

Some skills use a Large Language Model. No AI model is bundled with the App — you choose and connect one yourself:

  • If you use a local model (for example Ollama running on your own computer), your data never leaves your machine.
  • If you configure an external OpenAI-compatible or other third-party AI endpoint, then for those skills the relevant content (the text or data being processed, plus your instruction) is sent directly from your computer to that provider so it can return a result. It does not pass through the developer. That provider's handling of the data is governed by its own privacy policy and terms — please review them. You remain in control of which endpoint, if any, you connect.

6. No third-party sharing, ads, or sale of data

The App contains no advertising, no third-party trackers, and no data brokers. The developer does not sell, rent, or share your information — because the developer never receives it.

7. Security

Because processing is local, your data's security is primarily governed by the security of your own device. The developer recommends running the App only on a computer you trust and control, and safeguarding any local configuration file that contains API keys.

8. Children's privacy

The App is a general-purpose productivity tool and is not directed at children.

9. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the App evolves. The current version, with its “Last updated” date, is always available at this page's URL.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or the App can be raised on the project's GitHub page: github.com/harshalambani/platform-agnostic-skills-portable.

PA Skills Portable is free and open. This page is the canonical privacy statement for the application.
